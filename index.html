About

Overborne Games

The Name

Overborne. The name originates from "airborne," but it stood out for its uniqueness and, more importantly, for what it represents. "Overborne" feels like a step beyond simply being airborne. It suggests going further, rising higher, and pushing past limits. This perfectly reflects the identity.

Mission

An independent publishing company built for creative freedom. One flagship title receives dedicated live support. Other releases are tight, high-quality experiences patched as needed.

Philosophy

Player feedback is an essential guide — not an afterthought. We're dedicated to making great games and growing alongside our community. In time, the studio may expand into a small team to support more live-service titles.

The First Game

Development on Afrage began in 2024, with a target release date set for 2026. It is a first-person platformer currently in development, focusing on puzzle-solving and campaign progression.

Games

Afrage will be launching on Steam soon. The game is currently in the Steam approval process.
