ABOUT

The history about Overborne Games™ and the team.

The Start

It began with a single developer name Ali Abbasi. He worked alone late nights, quiet mornings, and a screen full of half-built worlds. Game development wasn't just a hobby, it was a promise to himself.

Founder of Overborne Games™

This didn't begin with paperwork, offices, or investors. It began with a low end computer, unstable internet, unstable electricity, and an obsession with games that felt unfinished. Ali don't want to just play games, he wanted to publish great games.

The Decision to Stand Alone

At some point, "solo game development" stopped feeling temporary. Instead of waiting for approval, funding, or a bigger studio, he decided to create his own game publishing company. That decision meant responsibility, marketing, branding, releases, and failures but also control.

Goals

The goal is simple, making good games that people love. Listening to the community and feedbacks, that's it!

The First Release

Afrage is coming soon on Steam. There will be a link for it.
