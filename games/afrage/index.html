About

Overborne Games is a video game industry made by Ali Abbasi in 2025.

What is Overborne Games?

In a fractured universe on the brink of collapse, you are the last Nexus Walker, gifted with the ability to traverse between dimensions. Your mission is to restore balance to the multiverse before it's torn apart by the Void Corruption.

Explore stunning realms, each with unique ecosystems, civilizations, and challenges. Forge alliances, master ancient powers, and make decisions that will echo across realities.

Open Multiverse

Travel seamlessly between 7 unique dimensions with their own ecosystems and civilizations.

Dynamic Companions

Recruit from 15+ unique characters with evolving relationships based on your choices.

Evolving Abilities

Master a skill tree with 200+ abilities that adapt to your playstyle.

AI-Driven Narrative

Experience a story that dynamically changes based on your decisions and actions.
